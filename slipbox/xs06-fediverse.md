# Fediverse

A term used to describe decentralzied services formally own, operated, and controlled by corporations.

People envision these technologies as free from the some of the effects pursuant to large companies like Facebook and Twitter and incentives for how these companies operate.

Examples:

- [Mastodon](https://mastodon.social/)
- [Lemmy](https://join-lemmy.org/)
- [Kbin](https://kbin.pub/)
